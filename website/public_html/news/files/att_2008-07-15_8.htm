CVUUF Bulletin Board

 

Purpose and Introduction

 

The purpose of the bulletin board is to inform members, friends and visitors of the activities sponsored by the Conejo Valley Unitarian Universalist Fellowship and other information of general interest to members of the Fellowship.  It is the intent of the CVUUF Board to balance the need for communications via posted information and displays with the desire to provide an aesthetically appealing and uncluttered appearance in our facility.

 

Policies

 

  • The CVUUF Board or its representative must approve all materials that are posted.  A suggested format will be provided for consistency and aesthetic purposes.  Materials must be given to this representative for approval, dating, and hanging.
  • The Board’s representative has final authority on the content and duration of all postings.
  • All postings should be no larger than 8" x 14".
  • Only one copy of each posting is allowed.
  • Each posting must include the name of a contact person and a requested expiration date.
  • Maintaining the timeliness of postings will be critical to both the effectiveness of communications and the clean appearance of the bulletin board. Postings may be removed due to lack of space. Postings advertising an event will be removed after the event has passed.
  • All materials will be disposed of after their dates have expired.
  • A master calendar listing all activities and events will be posted on a regular basis.
  • Violations of the poster policy will result in the posters being removed and discarded. We will make an effort to reach the contact persons and inform them why the poster was removed.
