MISSION/SCOPE OF COMMUNICATIONS DIRECTOR

 

 

  • Serve as consultant to Board, Directors, Committees, and other groups on how to communicate effectively using available communications tools.
    • Knowledge of tools and how to best use them
    • Development of forms, effective communications formats to enhance quality and ease of communications
    • Provide writing skills
    • Knowledge of timelines for communications
    • Who, what, when, where, and how

 

  • Develop policies for effective communications.

 

  • Assume ownership of bulletin boards.
    • Approval of content
    • Supporting policies
    • Timelines

 

  • Focus should be on content, policies, and consulting.

 

  • Goal should be to reach 100% of the target audience.
    • Who is included
    • Who is not included (provision for opting out)

 

  • Work closely with the Outreach Group and Operations Group to stay close to their efforts.

 

  • Develop good understanding of major communications efforts throughout the Fellowship by attending Council meetings and Board meetings as appropriate.

 

  • Exclusions:
    • Ownership of tools (with the exception of bulletin boards)
    • Responsibility for external communications, although consulting role could be of benefit
    • Ownership of content remains with Board, Council, Committees, etc.
    • Rental material and marketing efforts
