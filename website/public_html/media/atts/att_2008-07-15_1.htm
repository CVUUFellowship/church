Banking Resolution

The signers on all CVUUF Fellowship accounts are the President, Vice-President, Treasurer and Director of Finance. Two of the four signers are required to sign all checks.

The signers for the FYE June 30, 2008 were Jeff Goldstein, Bill Robinson, Terry Hinricher and James Bray. In a Fellowship meeting on June 8, 2008, Claudia Barton was elected President and Peter Schlaus was appointed Director of Finance. They replace Jeff Goldstein and James Bray as signers, effective July 1, 2008.
