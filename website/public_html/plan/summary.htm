Summary - or What will Neighborhood Church look like in five

years?

 

 

In five years Neighborhood Church will operate on an annual budget of 1.2 million dollars. There will be 750 adult members and 400 children. In addition to our current staff we will also employ a full time outreach director, a volunteer coordinator, and a third professional who Will lead a third worship service and mid-week service as well as coordinate small groups. Our traditional Sunday worship service will take place in a re-constructed sanctuary and will continue to offer spiritual renewal to all seekers. Our Congregation will connect with each other through intentional small groups, social events, committee work and outreach opportunities. Our facilities will be well used and well maintained. A planned giving program will be in place strengthening our effort to be a wellspring of liberal faith which brings together into a caring community people of varying beliefs, races, affectional orientations, ages, classes and cultures.

 

 

Implemenation

 

 

It is recommended that:

  • The Board of Trustees sponsor a series of discussion group opportunities in order to receive input from the congregation at-large as to what individuals like about this report, what they don’t like and what they feel is missing.
  • Once this report is revised and approved the Board of Trustees appoint an implementation committee to ensure that the recommendations brought forth in this report are implemented.
  • The Board of Trustees gives an annual progress report to the Congregation regarding the status of the implementation.
