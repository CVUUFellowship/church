Where We Are:

 

Our openness is just what the world needs right now and we are confident that others who hold these precious values will want to join with us on common ground.

The mission of Neighborhood Church is to serve the spirit, as each individual understands it, to serve the educational and pastoral needs of our own community, and to serve the larger world through actions that further social justice. The vision is for a church that will be a wellspring of liberal faith, which brings together into a caring community, people of varying beliefs, races, affectional orientations, ages, classes and cultures.

 

In recent years, the congregation of Neighborhood Church has designated itself as common ground. Our theological perspective, combined with our geographical location, makes our church the ideal community to bring together, in unity, people of wide differences. Our campus is common ground because it is a place:

• Where people of all ages meet one another in education and play.

• Where people of every race, economic class and culture join in to solve the problems of our larger world.

• Where people of all affectional orientations are welcomed into the fold of religious community.

• And where most importantly, people with varying beliefs come together in common worship.

Our openness is just what the world needs right now and we are confident that others who hold these precious values will want to join with us on common ground. Fulfilling a mission and vision does not lust happen”. It takes each member consciously contributing to, embracing and preserving our common wellspring of faith. It also takes a lot of hard work and planning.
