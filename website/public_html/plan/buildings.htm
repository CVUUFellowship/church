Buildings and Grounds

 

We should always have beautiful, well-maintained facilities; sufficient to meet the needs of the people and programs of Neighborhood Church, as well as outside organizations whose work we value.

We shall broaden and deepen our wellspring by providing:

 

An environment which grant refuge and Inspires us with the beauty of nature

 

Simply said the goal of Neighborhood Church regarding its buildings and grounds is to provide an environment which grants refuge and inspires us with the beauty of nature. We should always have beautiful, well-maintained facilities which are sufficient to meet the needs of the people and programs of Neighborhood Church, as well as outside organizations whose work we value.

 

To meet this goal we will need to resolve deferred and future maintenance issues. We will need to develop an integrated facilities development plan as well as a graphic development plan for the whole campus for the next five years and beyond. Furthermore, the Board of Trustees will need to institute a policy to review the facilities development plan at least annually. It is also important to be ever mindful of the technical aspects in the sanctuary that affect the quality of worship. Lighting & sound in the sanctuary should be a priority as we look at our maintenance needs and they need to be improved as soon as is feasible.

 

Neighborhood Church has added to the beauty and function of its campus with the completion of the new programs building. It is now time to begin looking at what has been termed ~phase II” of the building program. Phase II refers to the campus improvements planned for in the most recent building program but which have since been deferred. It is now time to address the condition of the sanctuary and patio, Neighborhood House and some additional landscaping areas. In the past few years the Church has learned how to successfully conduct a building program. It starts with a vision or dream of what the Congregation wants to see its place of worship become. Although it would be easy to refer back to the notes of a few years ago it is important to start anew as there are many newer members who were not here the last time we began to dream of a new sanctuary. It’s also possible, if not probable, that some of our wants and needs have changed from when we went through this process approximately 5 years ago.

 

We will want to consider this report in its entirety before we begin to attach a time frame to its implementation however, it is realistic to think that we would spend the first year visioning, prioritizing and planning a capital campaign. The second year would be spent raising the funds needed and the third year in construction.

 

The Church is fortunate to have an effective and willing Building Committee in existence which has expressed an interest in organizing the visioning/prioritizing process. Our recommendation would be to allow them to do so.

 

In addition, within the next five years the Church will need to consider what upgrades are necessary for the pre-school building and landscaping upgrades to areas not yet considered. There is also an interest in improving recreational facilities on our campus that will serve a wider age range.
